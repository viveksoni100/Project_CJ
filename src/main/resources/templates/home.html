Frequently Asked Questions

How can we integrate API to our application ?

You can access the API setup document in your account -> Subscription section. For any query, you can reach out to us over csupport@globalvoxinc.com

I am not finding the data list which i am looking for ?

Please share more details about your requirement to us over csupport@globalvoxinc.com. we will try to incorporate the same in our catalogue soon.

How to subscribe to a data list?

Select the data list which you wish to subscribe for, make payment and you can get access to your chosen data list.

How will we come to know about data list update?

As soon as we update any of the data list, all active subscribers would be notified over their registered email address.

Can I get any particular data list in my customized format ?

You may reach out to us over csupport@globalvoxinc.com with detailed format requirement. we will try to provide you the same data in your required format.
